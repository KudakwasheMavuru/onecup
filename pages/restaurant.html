Restaurant & Bar

Elevated Dining, Effortless Vibes

Where Flavor Meets Atmosphere

At One Cup Roasters, the experience doesn’t stop with great coffee. Step into our Restaurant & Bar, where every dish is a celebration of flavor and every drink tells a story. Whether you're here for a slow brunch, a quick business lunch, or a relaxed dinner with friends, we serve meals made with heart—and a hint of Rwandan soul.

Our Restaurant

We serve fresh, seasonal dishes made from locally sourced ingredients. Expect global flavors with a unique Rwandan twist. Vegetarian and vegan-friendly options are always on the menu. Perfect for casual meals, work lunches, or date nights.

The Bar

Sip on signature cocktails, curated wines, and local brews. Try our coffee-inspired mocktails and infused drinks. Join us for Happy Hour and unwind in style. A cozy, stylish space for relaxed evenings and great conversations.

Ambiance That Adapts

From sunlit morning energy to candlelit evenings, our restaurant and bar transform throughout the day. Expect a laid-back, elegant atmosphere, music that complements the mood, and staff that treat you like family.
