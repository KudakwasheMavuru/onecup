More Than Just Coffee.

It’s a One Cup Experience.

At One Cup Roasters, we believe that every great story begins with a great cup of coffee. Nestled in the heart of Kigali, we are more than a café — we’re a roastery, a community hub, and a celebration of Rwanda’s rich coffee heritage.

From the moment you step into our space, you’re stepping into a world crafted for connection, creativity, and quality. Whether you’re here to grab your morning brew, meet friends for brunch, or dive deep into a coffee-tasting journey, One Cup Roasters is where flavor meets experience.
